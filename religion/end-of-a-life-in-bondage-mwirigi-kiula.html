The book is about a person who was in the bondage of

lust. It was a difficult, long period fighting off. Mediocre

Christianity did not work. A moment in life came when

all the secret life of sin came to the open. Setting

down to a true Christian life, personal repentance,

discipleship, and wholesome commitment to the Word

of God brought regeneration. In a span of three years,

there was total change. The impact of the change was

evident and wholesome in all spheres of life including

the spiritual realm, family and community life, work and

service life, value system and relationships, stewardship

and leadership. The writer calls readers to enter into

the freedom that comes with accepting Jesus Christ as

Lord and Savior and living true to the Word of God as

the bedrock of an ultimate transformation.
