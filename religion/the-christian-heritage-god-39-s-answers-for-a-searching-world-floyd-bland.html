Have you ever wondered what the Christian life looks like in a perfect world? The Christian Heritage illustrates the Christian life through eight attributes, which when applied will: •Strengthen our faith in Christ •Help us live lives of moral excellence (virtue) •Show the importance of leaning God's Word through discipleship (knowledge) and then applying it through service •Demonstrate the importance of self-control (temperance) in the Christian life •Show how we can patiently endure to the end •Teach us the importance of godliness and how it is attained •Show how brotherly kindness can be expressed within the Christian community, and •Show how we can demonstrate God's unconditional love, which is the crowning glory of authentic Christianity Through the use of Scripture passages, interactive illustrations, and the works of other authors, The Christian Heritage is designed to help the reader understand how to live a Christ-centered, God-honoring life in our ever-changing world.
