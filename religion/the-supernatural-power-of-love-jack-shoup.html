We are comanded in Scripture to walk in love. However, if we do not comprehend what love truly is, then we will miss the mark.  Most have confused love with a feeling. Others believe that the epitome of love is to perform a few good deeds. However, to manifest the love of God transcends human ability.

The scriptures tell us that "God is love." In fact, the genuine love of God is supernatural in content and is the most powerful force in the universe.

This book outlines from scripture these supernatural qualities of love. It ties the love of God to His glory and relates what each will produce in the life of the believer.  As well, it unveils the spiritual principals that will inform us of how we can access this all-powerful force.

Without a doubt, if you will endeavor to follow the development path to love detailed in this book, you will be eternally changed.
