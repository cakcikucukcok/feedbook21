We have entered the 21st century and the Church of Jesus Christ is facing new realities and new challenges in this millennium. This anointed and appointed work will address certain areas of optimisation and innovation vitally needed by the Church of Jesus Christ to kingdomize the earth. My question for you is, Will you be part of the innovative Church that will change the destiny of the 21st Century?
