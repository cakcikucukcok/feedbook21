The path to achieving your unique divine assignment can be steep and shrouded with misery and difficulties. Not everyone will be Abraham, Joseph, Moses, Joshua, Esther, Deborah or David but your diligence in completing and staying focused on your divine task will earn you, your gift of infertility. GOD rewards! This book shares a candid account of the dynamic journey to embracing your divine assignment. From moments of conception, to moments of loss and to moments of fulfillment. Nothing should be held back as you battle towards reviving your destiny. A gift that is not only reflected in times of waiting to conceive a child but in any area of life including personal, career, business, marriage, ministry, leadership and relationships. The author is fulfilling a dream of becoming a writer and pens this book out of personal experience. It is simply the mind of GOD!
