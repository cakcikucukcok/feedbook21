Sound the Alarm –America is in trouble.

I love America. In God’s Providence, the world has never known a nation so rich, strong, and as good as America.  I thank God for our nation.  But America is in trouble. 

She is in trouble financially, politically, socially, militarily, and morally.  In the last few years there has been a rapid moral decay in our society.  We are spiritually sick and our condition is very serious.  This is a sickness that our politicians cannot solve.  

We once sought God to establish this nation on His eternal truths and morals.  Because of this, God blessed America.  However there is a new generation that has generally rejected the moral absolutes of God that were the foundation for our government, laws, education, and liberty.  God is grieved over our national sins.  

Many Bible believing Christians are grieved as well and are deeply concerned about the future of America.  This book examines the principles of God’s judgment of nations and seeks to answer the question: Will God judge America?  

This book is a call to hope and prayer that God will revive His Church and heal our nation before it is too late.   Come and “Stand in the gap” with us for our nation. 

Sound the Alarm - A Call to Prayer is a sample edition of 21 devotions from the Sound the Alarm, Does God Judge Nations? Will God Judge America? book.  The focus is on Hope and Intercession for America by including a section at the end of each devotion for you to include your prayers or thoughts of what God is saying to you personnally.
