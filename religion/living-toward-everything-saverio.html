Living Toward Everything is an honest and hopeful journey about where humanity stands in the quest to extend life.

It delves into the truths about what’s being done to increase human longevity with rejuvenated biology and how close we are to getting there.

After five years of dealing with end of life happenings with family and friends, the author used his lifelong developed talents to analyze the realities of Life Extension probabilities for those occupying the planet today. 

Life Extension is no longer a taboo subject in this world of quantum speed discovers that are helping to answer questions about age related illnesses and their cures.

Living with the possibility of indefinite health leads to the question of what humanity will do with the benefits of extended time on Earth.

The fast read journey is honest and often lighthearted with the author’s personal antidotes about trying to live happily while being aware that the end was not what any of us asked for!

Living Toward Everything also touches on the role of Religion in an everlasting human existence and what we might expect to be the ultimate discoveries in our future based on the “new direction for humanity” it presents.
