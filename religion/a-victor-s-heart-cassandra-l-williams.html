Often in life, we find ourselves focused on our histories, mired in the baggage of the pasta state that can keep us from living the lives that God intended. This devotional guide seeks to help you change your focus. Through these insights, you can stop the cycles that keep occurring in your life by dealing with the root cause and the ripples that followed a particular event, moment, or conversation. You can overcome your past; you are not what you have been through, unless you want to be.

A Victors Heart explores the four chambers of a personal journey toward God. Through love, forgiveness, and trust, you can reach an abundant life in God. As you pass through these chambers, discover the real strength that has been hiding beneath the surfacethe spiritual muscles that have been built by the events of your past. By sharing stories of her own life, author Cassandra Williams provides direction on how you can stop rehashing tragedies and instead move forward through Gods love.

Cycles were meant to be broken, and life was meant to be enjoyed. A Victors Heart offers the guidance you need to confront the problems keeping you from experiencing the abundant life God has ordained.
