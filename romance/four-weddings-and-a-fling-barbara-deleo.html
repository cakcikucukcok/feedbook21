After years as a private detective specializing in philandering spouses, Ari Katsalos avoids relationships, and family situations, wherever possible. But if he doesn't step in to help turn the fortunes of his parents' wedding hall business, The Aegean Palace, around, the Katsalos name, and everything his parents have worked for in the last thirty-five years will be lost.

Grace Bennett is determined to stick to her belief that there's a true soul-mate for everyone, and after establishing a successful business of her own, she'll have time to look for hers. But suddenly the wedding hall she runs is under attack and the devastatingly gorgeous bad boy, Ari steps in as her temporary boss begging her to stay and help him pull off a big society wedding that'll put everything right again. He'll even makes sure she gets all the credit.

With so much to do and so much riding on the success of this wedding, Grace and Ari have to learn to work together all while avoiding the strong chemistry between them, which is easier said than done.
