Maya has lost her sense of self. An underappreciated stay-at-home mom in an unhappy marriage, she embarks on a week-long solo vacation with the hope of finding herself again. But as she arrives in sultry North Florida, she thinks all that awaits her is umbrella drinks, warm sand, and a new perspective. Maya has no idea her entire life is about to change.

Maya just wants to feel young and sexy again. As she enjoys a drink at a tiki bar on her first night, she is lost in her thoughtsuntil a dashing man introduces himself. After Rafael and Maya share an intimate dance, they part for the night. Maya thinks it is nothing but an innocent attraction until two goons show up at her door a few minutes later and reveal they are holding her sister hostage. Suddenly, Maya is caught not only in a dangerous web of deception, but also a passionate affair with Rafaelwho is actually a CIA agent in the midst of a complicated case.

In this sexy, romantic tale, a woman lost in the demands of motherhood and a lifeless marriage sets out to find happiness and discovers her true self in the process.
