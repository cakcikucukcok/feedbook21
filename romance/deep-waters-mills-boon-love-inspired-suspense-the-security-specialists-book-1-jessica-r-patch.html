DUTY TO PROTECT

Thirty feet underwater when her oxygen tank fails, Caley Flynn fears it’s been sabotaged—and she'll be the next to die. Her intern’s already dead, her home breached, and something's amiss at her Florida marine-life rescue center…but no one believes she's in danger. Except Shepherd Lightman. Rescuing Caley and remaining alive isn’t easy, but resisting his boss's sister is where the real danger lies.
