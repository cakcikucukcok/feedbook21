When a devastating car crash dashes Parker Wood's dreams of being a pro baseball player, he returns to his childhood home to take over the family landscaping company. His dad may be gone now, but some things haven't changed. Like the crush Parker's always had on the girl next door.

Sophie Vaughn's two month marriage and hellishly public divorce didn't help her wedding and party planning business. She's determined not to let personal drama get in the way again, no matter how big the temptation, or how long she's lusted after her old best friend.

When Parker and Sophie discover they are neighbors once again, it's as if time has stood still. Their friendship is as strong as ever, as is the crush neither of them ever admitted having. They have a second chance at making their dreams come true, unless their secrets tear it apart.
