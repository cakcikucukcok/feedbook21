It's been a month since Sydney Parrish ended her relationship with Dylan Sims, the alluring man who masked her pain after her fiance's death. Although Sydney has work and nursing school to distract her thoughts, her feelings for Dylan won't seem to fade. Dylan's wife took her own life nearly five years ago, leaving him with indescribable pain and guilt. When Sydney walked into his life, he thought he'd finally be able to move on, he just never expected to fall in love so fast. Determined to get Sydney back, Dylan knows there are some changes he needs to make to be worthy of Sydney's affection and love. In this anticipated sequel to Life After Him: A Novella, find out if Sydney and Dylan can work through their losses and finally come together.
