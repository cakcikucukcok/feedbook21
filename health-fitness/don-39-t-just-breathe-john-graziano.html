What if you were to adjust your course in life while you are living it -- where you don't have to hit a wall before you change course?

After living a good portion of his life as an observer, author John Graziano began to reflect and look within himself. Eventually, realism kicked in. He grew aware that he, along with a good segment of our population, was living life with the constraints of social shackles. This caused him to view human behavior in an unconventional way.

Don't Just Breathe: Life Lessons from My Massage Table is a fundamental, straightforward approach to learn and laugh by examining our lives and emotional journeys using personal stories of inspiration that anyone can relate to -- no matter what age, gender, profession, or personality. It addresses a profound and prevalent phenomenon in our society: why so many people are unhappy. It gives glimpses into the lives of the author's clients and the struggles they face -- often through entertaining anecdotes. It encourages you to look at your own life with a fresh lens, and it may even spark the necessary lifestyle adjustments you need to make.

A portion of the proceeds from this book will be donated to the following: Integrative Touch for Kids, www.IntegrativeTouch.org and National Alliance on Mental Illness Southern Arizona (NAMI), www.namisa.org.
