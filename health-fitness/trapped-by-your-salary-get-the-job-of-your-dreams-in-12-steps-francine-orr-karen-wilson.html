Are you trapped in a job that doesn't suit or fit you? Do you wish you could move on but don't know where to start? Trapped By Your Salary will take you from Trapped By Your Salary to the Job Of Your Dreams in just 12 steps. This workbook is for anyone who is ready to move from where they are now to where they want to be. Combining techniques from Business and Personal Coaching, Neuro Linguistic Programming and Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Trapped By Your Salary is filled with insights and exercises to allow you to take control. Over 12 steps you will take a good look at yourself from your skills and abilities to how you dress, sound and present yourself. This is the journey of a lifetime.
