This book is about viewing the great Holy Scriptures through a single filter: That is, from the perspective of a purely loving and non-judgmental creator.

It takes and looks some of the common scriptures and re-interprets them and delves back to original meanings and intentions by looking back at the original sources from which they came:

The purpose that we are all here for is the pursuit of Truth

The challenge to mans pursuit of truth,

For we are at a great crossroad, is this;

Are we willing to ask the great questions?

Are we willing to challenge our most basic beliefs

that we have been telling ourselves fir thousands of years?

And that have led us inevitably to our state of current affairs?

Are we willing to seek, to uncover,

To discover truth?

This then is the path:

If we are to know, to discover

Then we must ask- question everything

And if we hear a voice telling us not to question a thing,

understand that is not gods voice, but mans.

As god rejoices in our seeking and will answer us,

For to seek truth, is to seek god.

And is our purpose.

Call to me, and I will answer you,

And will tell you great and unsearchable things

You do not know.

Jeremiah 33:3

For unless we ask

We will never discover our gentle god.

And until we understand our god

to be a purely good, unconditionally compassionate Creator

Man cannot see himself as good- in gods image

And cannot evolve to the gentle, compassionate being

That is our absolute destiny.

The earth shall be filled with the knowledge

of the benevolent goodness of the lord,

as the waters cover the sea.

Habakkuk 2:14
