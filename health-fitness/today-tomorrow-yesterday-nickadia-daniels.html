From odes in memory of her mother to poetry that educates younger readers about her heroes, Nickadia Daniels offers verse that is spiritually revolutionary. The distinctive tone of her work is informed by a legacy of unbridled promise and thwarted dreams. Today, Tomorrow, Yesterday is a collection of poetry that relays an empowering spirit of love, memorializing people of hope and moments of purpose. The passionate style of her work brings the trials and triumphs of her life and dreams to reality. Her poetry gives a view into her experiences covering a wide range of emotionslaughter, tears, triumph, and disappointmentand exploring the past, the present, and the future. Today, Tomorrow, Yesterday portrays experiences inspiring yet heartfelt sentiments that are universal to human existence.
