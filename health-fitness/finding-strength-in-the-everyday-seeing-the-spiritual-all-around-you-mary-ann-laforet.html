Finding Strength in the Everyday delivers positive messages in a daily format designed to be short and thought provoking. Each of the 366 devotions use either analogies, quotes, or biblical references. Each entry causes you to think about what is true for you or how to best live your life. The author deals with such themes as forgiveness, finding hope or joy, gardening, cooking, exercising, entertaining, friends, and even death. It is designed to be read by persons of all faiths, not just Christians. You will want to keep this book next to your morning coffee or on your bedside table to enable you to read one brief selection each day in order to find the spiritual all around you.
