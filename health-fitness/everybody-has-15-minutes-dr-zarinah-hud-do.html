When chronic pain becomes a part of your everyday experience, it is hard to believe that food could be the key to pain relief. This book will help you rebound your health by providing: 

  • Breakfast, lunch, dinner and dessert recipes 

  • Kid-friendly options 

  • Helpful hints and tips to ensure a tasty meal 

With delicious options like Curry Chickpea Rice and Flounder, Chili Lime Cod in Creamy Garlic Sauce, and Reese’s Cup Smoothie, you too can gain pain freedom while enjoying every bite!
