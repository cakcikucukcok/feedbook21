Too many times we as Women look to improve ourselves from the outside in , when God urges us to Grow and love ourselves from the inside out. and to appreciate his love for us and Blessings,

Godly Women and Lipstick is written to inspire women of all ages and backgrounds, and to remind us that we can only help others when we first help ourselves, we can only grow stronger when we embrace and love ourselves , inside and out, as God made us.

Sheila Woolum puts God first in all things , and wants to help others do the same. She seeks to build a ministry with love , hope and kindness to help those less fortunate. especially the youth. she hopes to start a home for Children and teenagers and looks forward to helping young people, both abroad and in her own community. Sheila's prayer is to lead the way for others to serve God, and to have a kind heart for those who are lost , weak or without hope.
