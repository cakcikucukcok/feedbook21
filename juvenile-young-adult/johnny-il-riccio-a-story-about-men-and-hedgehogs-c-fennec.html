"Johnny il riccio" (Johnny the hedgehog) is a novel for children who grow up and ask, for children who overlook the delicate door of adolescence with curiosity and intelligence, looking for answers to their first unknowns of life. But it is also the perfect book to read to their children when they are still young and listen to everything with incredible attention and wonderful infinite availability, because it is fun, ironic, fantastic … "Johnny il riccio" is a metaphor of life that teaches you to believe in love and friendship, which teaches perseverance and determination, which teaches courage and redemption. And it teaches to respect the hedgehogs that too often suddenly end up under the wheels of our cars. After reading it, every hedgehog will be Johnny ..!
