This book is about Paisley the dog. This is book one in the series about Paisley. Paisley came to Micheles home as a broken, helpless little puppy that had been abused and had nowhere to live. In this story, Paisley takes in characters who, like herself, have found themselves homeless and facing bleak futures. It is a story about family, acceptance, respect, sharing, caring, and love. Paisley becomes a mentor who leads everyone to a place where they can live the lives they choose and be happy. She is such a good puppy dog, she is personally selected from all the other puppy dogs by Santa to lead his team. This beautiful story will fill your hearts with love. The message is to be good to one another and love one another, and together the world can become a better place for everyone.
