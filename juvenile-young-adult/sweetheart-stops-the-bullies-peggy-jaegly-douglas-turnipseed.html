Though Sweetheart thinks he is teaching his new friend how to enjoy life, Bob astounds all the animals and humans on the farm when he reveals his unique talent.

With illustrations by Douglas Turnipseed, Sweetheart Stops the Bullies is the latest in the Sweet Pony series, which has been praised by parents and teachers. Peggys other books include: Sweethearts Gift and Sweethearts Treasure Hunt.
