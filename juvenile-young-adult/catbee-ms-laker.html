This book is about imagination; about having fun within the world we live in by exploring the mind and setting no limits to what we can do or be. This book creates silly characters that are not real for this world, but that will get a child to use their imagination and understand that laughter is important in all cultures and languages. If you can imagine things that are unknown or unreal, then you can imagine things that could be. The mind is limitless and should always be explored. This book teaches children that imagination is essential and laughter is universal.
