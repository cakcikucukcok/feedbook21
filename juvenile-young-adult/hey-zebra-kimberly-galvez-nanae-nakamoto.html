Growing up multiracial, I struggled with my ethnic identity. I was teased as a child and often called a zebra. In my young adult life, I went to school for early-childhood and elementary education. I was inspired to write this book for children to let them know that despite their differences, they are all special. Creating Hey Zebra allowed me to take a word that I once viewed as a negative and turn it into a positive.
