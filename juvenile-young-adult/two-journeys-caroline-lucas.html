An American billionaire threatens to destroy an Eastern England fens' National Nature Reserve with a golf course. Local farmer's son Josh, doctor's disabled daughter Beverley, and youngsters Tim and Ben, form the Fenland Knights secret society.

Politicians ignore the reserve's protected status, forcing the Knights into action. A rare bittern inhabits Little Mere - restored by Josh and elder brother Tom - so when Tom goes missing in Afghanistan it becomes personal. Beverley hatches several clever endeavours, but sadly, they mostly suffer set-backs. However, when Ben and Tim's good deed saves a little dog: have they at last stumbled upon an answer?
