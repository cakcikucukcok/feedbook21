What’s the best SMALL pet for you?

What pets are the most interesting? And which ones are the most FUN?

This chapter book that tells you what it’s like to have the best pets for kids 8 to 12.

These creatures are all good choices for beginning pet owners because these animals are:

 Friendly and tame. They’re bred to be pets

  • Easy to care for and keep healthy with minimal adult supervision
  • Proven to be kid-friendly
  • Pleasant to live with
  • Widely available
  • Not expensive to buy or maintain
  • FUN for kids, or anyone who wants a pet

No one wants a pet that is a lot of work, or makes noise all the time, or destroys things, or doesn’t like people or is boring!

Before you pick your pet – or your next pet – have a look at these best pets for kids. Even if you don’t end up getting one (or more) of these appealing pets, you’ll enjoy reading about them!

This is a Chapter book written on the Grade Four Reading Level

Includes over 20 photos of small and fun pets.

Curious kids who enjoy knowing more about the pets they want or already have will enjoy this animal book.

This is a fun book for kids, or for anyone who intrigued by animals and the natural world.
