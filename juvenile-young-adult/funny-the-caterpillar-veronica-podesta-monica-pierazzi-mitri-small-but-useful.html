EXCERPT

Funny is a caterpillar and a dreamer. The other night, she dreamt she was climbing to the top of the mountain. Without a second thought she decided to make her dream come true. Along the way she bumped into Mr Frog, who laught and said:

- Don’t even think about it! I am a very agile frog and so can jump and reach the summit of the mountain but you... a caterpillar who crawls so slowly... it’s crazy! 

He went away jumping and bloated like a frog.
