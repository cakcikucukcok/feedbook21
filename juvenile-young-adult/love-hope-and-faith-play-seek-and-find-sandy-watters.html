The horses in our storybook, Love and Hope, are such an enrichment to my familys lives, I would like to share with you the information about Bit of Hope Rescue Ranch where they were adopted from.

Bit of Hope gives horses a second chance at life. They are rescued and teamed with young children from the community to love them, help train and care for them. The rewards for both horse and child are immeasurable. Please visit their website at: http://www.bitofhoperanch.com/about

All donations are much needed and welcome!
