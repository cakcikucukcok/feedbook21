DREW THE DANCING DUCK is a great teaching tool for children ages of three to nine years old. Drew was created to give children a friendly character they could identify with. He is an ordinary-looking duck with a jovial attitude. In this book Drew demonstrates how he is so appreciative of the good and positive things in his life.

 

DREW THE DANCING DUCK emphasizes the importance of being observant. He celebrates all the things he appreciates in his life by dancing with happiness. Drew learns and shares with the readers that there are many things to be appreciative of and encourages children not to be afraid to show their happiness. Dancing is Drew’s way of expressing happiness and appreciation.

Through the eyes of a cute duck, author Stephanie Simpson encourages kids to show gratitude for what is going great in the child’s life. Using the character of a duck and colorful illustrations, the author is able to give an example of how to have a positive outlook on the good things that life does offer. The purpose of this book is to inspire and make a difference in a child’s life, one message at a time.
