The HiSet® Exam (High School Equivalency Test) is an alternative to the GED® Test that's currently offered in a dozen states, with more set to adopt it soon. Students looking to prepare for this test will find valuable information about the content of the test, the test-day experience, and how to best prepare for every question. Peterson's Master the HiSET® provides detailed reviews of the language arts-reading, language arts-writing, mathematics, science, and social studies tests, along with a diagnostic test to help students determine their strengths and weaknesses. Inside you'll also find 2 full-length practice tests PLUS access to 2 online practice tests-all with detailed answer explanations. Additional practice is also included to improve vocabulary and critical-thinking skills.
